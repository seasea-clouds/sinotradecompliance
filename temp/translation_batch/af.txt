What is GACC Decree 248?
GACC Decree 248 is a Chinese regulation requiring all overseas food manufacturers to register with the General Administration of Customs of China (GACC) before exporting food products to China. It applies to all 18 food categories and took effect January 1, 2022.

What is CCC certification?
CCC (China Compulsory Certification) is a mandatory safety certification for products sold in China. It covers electronics, automotive parts, toys, safety glass, and other product categories listed in the CCC catalog.
Does my product need CCC certification?
Products listed in China's CCC catalog require certification before they can be imported, sold, or used commercially. We provide a free catalog assessment to determine if your products fall under CCC requirements.
How long does CCC certification take?
The CCC process typically takes 4-8 months including factory audit, product testing at CNAS-accredited labs, and certification review. Timeline varies by product category and complexity.

What is NMPA cosmetics filing?
NMPA (National Medical Products Administration) filing is required for all cosmetics imported into China. General cosmetics require notification filing, while special-use cosmetics (sunscreen, hair dye, whitening) require registration approval.
Can I sell cosmetics through cross-border e-commerce without NMPA?
Yes, cross-border e-commerce (CBEC) channels allow cosmetics sales without NMPA filing under certain conditions. This is a faster market entry route while you prepare full NMPA compliance.

What is the Madrid System?
The Madrid System allows you to extend an existing trademark registration to multiple countries, including China, through a single application. It can be faster and more cost-effective than direct filing in China.
